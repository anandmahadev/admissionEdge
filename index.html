01About Us

AdmissionEdge was founded in 2012 by educators who watched too many bright students end up in the wrong college — not because of poor marks, but because of poor guidance. We're based in Mangalore, one of South India's most important education hubs, and we've spent over a decade studying every campus and admission process in this region.

We work with students at every stage — from choosing the right stream after Class 10, to navigating NEET counselling, to helping working professionals find the right MBA. Every student gets a personal counsellor who stays with them until the admission letter is in hand.

We don't push colleges. We don't take hidden commissions. What we have is a deep, honest understanding of what each college offers and what each student truly needs.
